## About Me

I am a researcher working at the intersection of **computational psychiatry**, **cognitive neuroscience**, and **intelligent computing**. My work focuses on understanding the neural and computational mechanisms underlying mental health and complex human behavior.

Using cutting-edge neuroimaging techniques (MRI/fMRI) combined with computational modeling and machine learning, I investigate how the brain supports cognition and how disruptions in these systems give rise to psychiatric disorders.

**I am always looking for motivated students and collaborators.** If you are interested in joining my lab or exploring potential collaborations, please feel free to reach out.
